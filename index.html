Jimin of BTS releases second solo album MUSE.

Jimin of 21st century pop icons BTS is set to release his second solo album MUSE on July 19, 2024. While FACE delved into confronting one’s inner self, through MUSE, Jimin candidly shares the stories he wished to convey while portraying his journey of finding inspiration from his surroundings.
Jimin, who led the overall production of MUSE, tells the story of making various attempts at finding real, genuine love and losing his way at times over the album’s seven tracks: “Rebirth (Intro),” “Interlude : Showtime,” “Smeraldo Garden Marching Band (feat. Loco),” “'Slow Dance (feat. Sofia Carson),” “Be Mine,” “'Who,” and “Closer Than This.” The theme of love begins with the first track “Rebirth (Intro)” and gradually unfolds throughout the album, reaching its climax at the main track “Who.”
